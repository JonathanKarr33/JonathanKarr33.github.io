Combating Homelessness Stigma with LLMs: A New Multi-Modal Dataset for Bias Detection
Jonathan A. Karr Jr., Benjamin F. Herbst, Ting Hua, Matthew Hauenstein, Georgina Curto, Nitesh V. Chawla
arXiv v2 Local v3 Code Dataset

Abstract: This research introduces methods leveraging large language models (LLMs) to identify and measure social bias against people experiencing homelessness (PEH) in digital spaces. The study presents a manually-annotated multi-modal dataset compiled from various online sources, providing evidence of homelessness bias typologies.
