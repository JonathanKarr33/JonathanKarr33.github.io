Technology and Catholicism Club of Notre Dame


The Technology and Catholicism Club of Notre Dame aims to explore the realm of technology as it relates to catholicism, create awareness, and develop student fellowship by having regular discussions, presentations, and speakers. The club is open to all students, regardless of their major or background. We welcome anyone who is interested in exploring the intersection of technology and faith, and we encourage you to join us for our discussions and events. Our Weekly discussions during the school year are held on Tuesdays at 7:00 PM in Como 216C, more info can be found here or email us at techcath@nd.edu.
